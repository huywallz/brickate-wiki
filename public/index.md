# Brickate Wiki
Brickate is a high-performance 3D sandbox platform for creators who love coding and blocky, stud-based games.

By combining a professional-grade "bare bones" API with the power of [LuaJIT](http://luajit.org/luajit.html), Brickate gives you the freedom to build complex simulations and multiplayer experiences with near-native execution speeds.

The Brickate Wiki is a work in progress. Many sections are still being updated to match the latest engine features.

## Lua
Never used Lua before? It's a great language! While this manual doesn't teach Lua itself, there are many excellent resources available to help you get started.

* [Lua.org](https://www.lua.org/): The official home of the language.
* [Programming in Lua (PiL)](https://www.lua.org/pil/): The definitive guide by the creators.
* [Learn Lua in 15 Minutes](https://learnxinyminutes.com/docs/lua/): A fast-paced overview for developers.
